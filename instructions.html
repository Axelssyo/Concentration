How to play ?

  1. Cards are shuffled and faced down.
  2. Chose the first card and click on it. The card will flip over and reveal its color.
  3. Chose the second card and click on it. The card will flip over and reveal its color.
  4. If both cards are the same colored, then the cards will disappear. But if the colors are not the same the cards will flip over to their back.
  5. Collect all pairs to win.
